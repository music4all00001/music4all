Meet the Facilitators

About Praiseaddict

Oladipo Adebayo Ebiesuwa, (pka Praiseaddict), is a dedicated worship and praise leader with a longstanding commitment to the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) since 1998. He began his journey as a choir member in the children and teens' choir, progressing into the main adult choir. Praiseaddict's musical journey was nurtured from an early stage under the guidance of his first music director, Funmilayo Idowu. In 2005, he furthered his musical education through a collaboration between RCCG LPXI and The Musical Society of Nigeria (Muson) Centre. Additionally, he had the privilege of participating in "Voice it Out," a music (voice) masterclass facilitated by renowned vocal coach Segun Obe. This opportunity, sponsored twice by his then-pastor, Pastor Danjuma Tafawa-Balewa, provided him with invaluable tutelage from esteemed music artists such as Tim Godfrey, Onos Ariyo, Timi Dakolo, and Segun Obe, among others. During his tenure as a teenage music director at RCCG LPXI, Adebayo demonstrated a track record of training church members in the foundational principles of music, including music theory and instrument proficiency. He continued this work at various RCCG branches, including Glorious Chapel, LPXI, Cornerstone Parish (My Father's House), LP 15 Headquarters, and the Redeemed Christian Fellowship (RCF) at Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba (AAUA) Chapter during his university years. Praiseaddict's unwavering dedication to music stems from his fervent desire to offer the highest quality musical worship to God on every possible occasion. This commitment birthed "Music4All", a platform aimed at simplifying the understanding of music fundamentals for musicians of all levels.

About Soye Aguda

Soye is an exceptional gospel artist with a rich background as a lead vocalist, songwriter, music director, and mentor. Her passion for music was ignited during her childhood and teenage years, where she quickly rose to prominence by taking on lead roles in various choir groups. Over the years, Soye has had the honour of ministering alongside renowned artists such as James Numbere, Precious Emmanuel, Precious Marc, Ijay Grigs Bartels, and Nsisong, among others. Currently, Soye serves as a key figure in Living Word Voices, the choir arm of The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), Living Word Centre in Darlington, UK. Her leadership extends across multiple choir groups, including King Sceptre Youth Church and RCCG Kingdom Chapel, where she has excelled as an executive, lead vocalist, and mentor. Soye's deep commitment to nurturing the talents of young people has driven her to train numerous individuals to harness their vocal potential and develop leadership skills in worship. Her influence spans both Nigeria and the UK, where she has successfully mentored and developed several groups of gospel singers. One of Soye's notable recent engagements is her collaboration as a co-trainer with Oladipo Adebayo Ebiesuwa (aka Praiseaddict) for Music4All, a non-profit initiative inspired by a divine calling, dedicated to the continuous development of church choirs across the UK. Through her passion, dedication, and expertise, Soye continues to make a significant impact on the gospel music scene, both locally and internationally.
